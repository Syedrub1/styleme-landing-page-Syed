About StyleMe: Revolutionizing Fashion

StyleMe is an AI-powered personalized fashion assistant designed to solve the common problem of choosing the right outfits for various occasions. End-users often struggle with outfit combinations, leading to embarrassment, impacting professional outcomes, and contributing to financial and resource waste from rarely worn clothes.

Our vision is to revolutionize the fashion industry by providing innovative digital solutions that simplify wardrobe management and promote sustainable fashion choices. We empower individuals to effortlessly curate stylish outfits from their existing wardrobe, reducing decision fatigue, saving time, and promoting environmental sustainability.

This capstone project was developed by **Syed, Avishek, and Ranjit** from the Institute of Product Leadership. We aim to provide a comprehensive and effective solution for budget-conscious college students, busy professionals, fashion-forward trendsetters, and eco-conscious consumers.
